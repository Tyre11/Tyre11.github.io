Travel Plan, December 28, 2020

I am planning a trip to travel to the West Coast and see the Pacific Ocean and a few other sights along the way! Starting from Bloomington the route: Grand Island NE, Boulder CO, Salt Lake City UT, Crescent City CA, Santa Cruz CA, Los Angeles CA, Las Vegas NV, The Grand Canyon, Albuquerque NM, Dallas TX then back to Bloomington. The Google Maps doesn't 100% reflect this as I hit the route spot limitation. I will make sure to update this if the weather get to nasty for my Toyota Corolla I may head south sooner.
